Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyCopilot

How does ReplyCopilot work?

+

Simply take a screenshot of any conversation, share it to ReplyCopilot via iOS Share Sheet, choose your tone (Professional, Friendly, Funny, or Flirty), and get 3-5 smart reply suggestions in under 2 seconds. You can then copy or insert the reply using our custom keyboard.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Absolutely! We have a strict zero data retention policy. Screenshots are processed in-memory only and immediately deleted after generating replies. We never store screenshots, message content, or any personally identifiable information. All data is encrypted in transit with TLS 1.3.

What messaging apps does it work with?

+

ReplyCopilot works with ANY messaging app! We have specific optimizations for WhatsApp, iMessage, Instagram, Outlook, Slack, and Microsoft Teams, but it works universally with Facebook Messenger, Telegram, Signal, Discord, Twitter DMs, TikTok, Snapchat, email clients, SMS, and more.

Can I try it before paying?

+

Yes! We offer a generous free tier with 20 replies per day. No credit card required. You can upgrade to Pro ($9.99/month) anytime for unlimited replies and advanced features. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

How accurate are the AI-generated replies?

+

Very accurate! We use OpenAI's GPT-4o Vision, the most advanced multimodal AI model. It understands context, tone, relationship dynamics, and platform-specific communication styles. Our users report a 95%+ satisfaction rate with reply quality.

Do I need to install anything besides the iOS app?

+

No! Everything works through the iOS app, Share Extension, and optional custom keyboard. No browser extensions, no desktop software. Just install the app from the App Store and you're ready to go.

What's the difference between tones?

+

Professional: Formal business language for work emails, Slack, Teams.
Friendly: Warm, casual tone for friends, family, social media.
Funny: Playful, humorous responses with jokes and wit.
Flirty: Charming, suggestive language for dating apps and romantic interests.

Can I cancel anytime?

+

Yes, absolutely! Cancel your subscription anytime from the app settings or iTunes. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period, then automatically revert to the free tier.
